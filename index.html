Failure Patterns

Most failures appeared supportive

Safety failures did not appear hostile or overtly harmful. They appeared helpful — while causing harm. This is the detection problem.

📊
Reinforcing distress instead of regulating it
Validating and amplifying negative emotional states while appearing empathetic and engaged.
🌀
Accelerating rumination loops
Engaging repeatedly with repetitive negative thought patterns instead of gently redirecting them.
⚠
Missing escalation signals
Failing to detect when a conversation requires professional intervention, crisis protocols, or referral.
💥
Amplifying emotional intensity
Increasing the emotional charge of interactions, escalating toward dependency or dysregulation.
